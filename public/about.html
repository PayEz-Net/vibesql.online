About VibeSQL

The database built for agents. We're building the complete abstraction of the data plane — governable, open, and vendor-neutral.

AI agents need a data layer that speaks their language. Not SQL strings cobbled together by prompt engineering. Not vendor-locked managed services that hold your data hostage.

VibeSQL is the complete abstraction of the data plane — a novel JSON-based SQL platform with governable encryption, multi-tenant isolation, and native Agent Communication Protocol support. Open source, Apache 2.0, deploy anywhere.

We believe the future of data is agent-driven, and that future should be open.

Core Pillars

The principles that drive every decision we make.

🔐

Governable Encryption

You control your encryption keys, your governance policies, your compliance posture. Not us. Not your cloud provider. You.

🔓

No Vendor Lock-in

Apache 2.0. Fork it, host it, white-label it. Your data lives on your infrastructure. Move it whenever you want. No exit fees, no proprietary formats.

🤖

Agent Communication Protocol

Native ACP support means your agents coordinate through the database, not around it. Async messaging, state management, and multi-agent orchestration built in.

📡

Data Plane Abstraction

The complete abstraction of the data plane. Your agents interact with JSON schemas and JSON queries — the underlying storage engine is invisible and replaceable.
